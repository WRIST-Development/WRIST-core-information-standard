## User Scenarios ### Scenario 1: New Patient Registration A person moves to a new area and registers with a GP practice. The practice administrator needs to capture comprehensive demographic information and obtain consent for information sharing. | Category | Description | |---|---| | Actors | GP Practice Administrator (CISV2.P1), Person (CISV2.P6) | | Data Elements | Person demographics, NHS number, contact details, consent | | Process | Registration workflow (CISV2.BP1) | | Outcome | Complete patient record established with appropriate consents | ### Scenario 2: Hospital Admission with Complex Needs An elderly person with multiple conditions is admitted to hospital. The clinical team needs to access their care preferences, medication history, and any advance decisions. | Category | Description | |---|---| | Actors | Hospital Clinician (CISV2.P2), Person/Carer (CISV2.P6) | | Data Elements | Problem list, medications, individual requirements, legal information | | Process | Admission assessment workflow (CISV2.BP2) | | Outcome | Comprehensive admission assessment with person-centred care plan | ### Scenario 3: Safeguarding Concern A community nurse identifies potential safeguarding issues during a home visit. They need to document concerns and initiate appropriate referrals. | Category | Description | |---|---| | Actors | Community Nurse (CISV2.P3), Social Worker (CISV2.P4) | | Data Elements | Safeguarding indicators, risk assessment, professional contacts | | Process | Safeguarding referral workflow (CISV2.BP3) | | Outcome | Safeguarding concern documented and referred appropriately |
