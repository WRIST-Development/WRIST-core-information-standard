## Implementation Team Personas | Persona ID | Occupational Title | Description | Targeted | Key Competencies | | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | | CISV2.P1 | Clinical Information Manager | Healthcare professional responsible for clinical data governance and standards implementation | Yes | Clinical knowledge, data standards, information governance | | CISV2.P2 | Health IT System Analyst | Technical analyst specialising in healthcare system integration and data mapping | Yes | FHIR, HL7, healthcare interoperability, system analysis | | CISV2.P3 | Clinical Safety Officer | Medical professional ensuring patient safety aspects of digital implementations | Yes | Clinical risk management, patient safety, regulatory compliance | | CISV2.P4 | Data Protection Officer | Privacy specialist managing GDPR compliance and data sharing governance | Yes | Data protection law, privacy impact assessment, consent management | | CISV2.P5 | Implementation Project Manager | Project manager coordinating cross-functional teams for standard deployment | Yes | Healthcare project management, change management, stakeholder engagement | ### Clinical Information Manager (CISV2.P1) The Clinical Information Manager serves as the bridge between clinical practice and technical implementation. They ensure that data structures accurately reflect clinical workflows and that coded terminology aligns with established practices. Their expertise in both healthcare delivery and information governance makes them essential for validating that the standard meets clinical needs while maintaining data quality. ### Health IT System Analyst (CISV2.P2) Health IT System Analysts translate clinical requirements into technical specifications. They possess deep understanding of healthcare interoperability standards and are responsible for mapping existing system data to the Core Information Standard format. Their technical expertise ensures seamless integration with existing NHS digital infrastructure. ### Clinical Safety Officer (CISV2.P3) Clinical Safety Officers evaluate patient safety implications of implementing the standard. They conduct clinical risk assessments, ensure that information flows support safe clinical decision-making, and validate that critical safety information is appropriately captured and communicated across care settings. ### Data Protection Officer (CISV2.P4) Data Protection Officers ensure that implementation complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations. They oversee consent management processes, conduct privacy impact assessments, and establish governance frameworks for appropriate information sharing while protecting individual privacy rights. ### Implementation Project Manager (CISV2.P5) Implementation Project Managers coordinate the complex, multi-stakeholder process of deploying the standard across healthcare organisations. They manage timelines, resources, and stakeholder communications while ensuring that implementation remains aligned with strategic objectives and clinical priorities.
