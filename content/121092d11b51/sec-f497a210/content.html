# User Scenarios ## Scenario 1: New Patient Registration and Assessment **Description:** A new patient attends their GP practice for registration and initial health assessment. **Primary Persona:** Clinical Practitioner (CISV2.P1) **Supporting Personas:** Administrative Staff (CISV2.P4), Service User (CISV2.P5) **Process:** Initial Patient Registration (CISV2.BP1) | Data Category | Description | |---|---| | Person Demographics | Full name, date of birth, address, contact details, NHS number | | GP Practice Registration | Practice details, registration date, preferred GP | | About Me Information | Patient's self-description of what's important to them | | Individual Requirements | Reasonable adjustments, communication needs | | Medical History | Previous conditions, family history, current medications | | Social Context | Household composition, occupation, lifestyle factors | ## Scenario 2: Care Plan Review and Update **Description:** A care coordinator reviews and updates a person's care and support plan following a multi-disciplinary team meeting. **Primary Persona:** Care Coordinator (CISV2.P2) **Supporting Personas:** Clinical Practitioner (CISV2.P1), Social Worker (CISV2.P3), Service User (CISV2.P5) **Process:** Care Plan Management (CISV2.BP2) | Data Category | Description | |---|---| | Goals and Actions | Updated goals, action plans, responsible parties | | Risk Assessment | Current risks, mitigation strategies, review dates | | Service Provision | Active services, funding arrangements, contact details | | Professional Networks | Key workers, specialist contacts, referral status | | Review Scheduling | Next review date, responsibility for coordination | ## Scenario 3: Emergency Care Access **Description:** Emergency services need rapid access to critical patient information during an urgent care episode. **Primary Persona:** Clinical Practitioner (CISV2.P1) in emergency setting **Supporting Personas:** Service User (CISV2.P5), Family Carer (CISV2.P6) **Process:** Emergency Information Access (CISV2.BP3) | Data Category | Description | |---|---| | Critical Alerts | Allergies, high-risk medications, safety concerns | | Emergency Contacts | Next of kin, key professionals, emergency preferences | | Current Medications | Active prescriptions, dosages, recent changes | | Advance Decisions | CPR status, advance directives, preferred place of care | | Key Health Issues | Active problems, recent procedures, ongoing treatments |
