## Introduction The Core Information Standard Version 2 (CISV2) defines the essential information elements required for effective health and care delivery across the NHS and social care settings. This standard ensures consistent capture and sharing of person-centred information to support integrated care. ### Purpose and Scope This Digital Adaptation Kit (DAK) provides implementation guidance for CISV2, enabling system developers and healthcare organisations to adopt the standard effectively. The scope encompasses demographic details, clinical information, care planning, legal frameworks, and social care elements required for comprehensive person-centred records. ### Background Developed through extensive stakeholder consultation, CISV2 builds upon established NHS data standards while incorporating person-centred care principles. The standard addresses the need for interoperable records that support both clinical decision-making and person-directed care planning across health and social care boundaries.
