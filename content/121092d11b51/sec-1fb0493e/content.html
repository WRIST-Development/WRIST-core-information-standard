## Generic Personas The following personas represent key users and stakeholders of the Core Information Standard v2.0 implementation. | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | CISV2.P1 | GP/Primary Care Physician | Manages ongoing care for patients in community settings; needs comprehensive view of medical history, medications, and social circumstances | Clinical assessment, medication management, care coordination, SNOMED CT coding | | CISV2.P2 | Social Worker | Coordinates social care services and safeguarding; requires access to legal information, care plans, and support networks | Safeguarding assessment, care planning, Mental Capacity Act knowledge, multi-agency coordination | | CISV2.P3 | Community Nurse | Provides nursing care in home/community settings; needs medication information, care plans, and communication preferences | Clinical procedures, medication administration, wound care, patient education | | CISV2.P4 | Hospital Consultant | Specialist providing acute/specialist care; requires clinical history, investigation results, and advance decisions | Specialist clinical knowledge, diagnostic interpretation, treatment planning | | CISV2.P5 | Care Home Manager | Oversees residential care delivery; needs comprehensive personal information, care plans, and legal documentation | Care quality management, staff supervision, regulatory compliance | | CISV2.P6 | Patient/Service User | Person receiving care; wants control over personal information sharing and care preferences | Self-advocacy, health literacy (variable), personal preference expression | | CISV2.P7 | Family Carer | Provides informal care and advocacy; needs understanding of care plans and communication methods | Care provision, advocacy, emotional support, care coordination | | CISV2.P8 | Paramedic/Emergency Responder | Provides urgent/emergency care; requires rapid access to critical information including allergies and advance decisions | Emergency assessment, rapid decision-making, clinical procedures | ### Persona Context These personas interact with different aspects of the Core Information Standard based on their roles. GPs and consultants focus on clinical sections, while social workers emphasize legal frameworks and support needs. All personas benefit from the person-centered "About Me" information for delivering appropriate care.
