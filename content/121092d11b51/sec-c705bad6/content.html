## Key User Personas The following personas represent the primary users of systems implementing the Core Information Standard: | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | GP/Primary Care Physician | Provides comprehensive primary care and coordinates patient care | Clinical assessment, care coordination, prescribing | | CISV2.P2 | Community Nurse | Delivers nursing care in community settings | Clinical assessment, care planning, medication management | | CISV2.P3 | Social Worker | Assesses social care needs and coordinates support services | Needs assessment, care planning, safeguarding | | CISV2.P4 | Hospital Consultant | Provides specialist medical care in acute settings | Specialist diagnosis, treatment planning, clinical leadership | | CISV2.P5 | Care Home Manager | Oversees residential care provision | Care oversight, staff management, regulatory compliance | | CISV2.P6 | Mental Health Professional | Provides specialist mental health assessment and treatment | Mental health assessment, therapeutic intervention, risk assessment | | CISV2.P7 | Administrative Staff | Manages patient records and administrative processes | Data entry, record management, appointment scheduling | | CISV2.P8 | Emergency Services Clinician | Provides urgent and emergency care | Rapid assessment, emergency treatment, critical decision making | Each persona has specific information needs and workflows that the standard must accommodate, from detailed clinical documentation to streamlined emergency access.
