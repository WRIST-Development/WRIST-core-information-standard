## Clinical Information Management Processes The Core Information Standard supports structured healthcare workflows that ensure person-centred care delivery while maintaining clinical safety and regulatory compliance. These processes define how clinical teams capture, validate, and utilise comprehensive health information across care settings. ### Process Design Principles - **Person-centred approach**: All processes prioritise individual preferences and involve patients in decision-making - **Clinical safety**: Critical information such as allergies and risks must be captured and communicated effectively - **Information governance**: Data handling complies with GDPR and clinical information governance standards - **Interoperability**: Structured data capture enables seamless information sharing between systems - **Professional accountability**: Clear assignment of clinical responsibility for data accuracy and care decisions These processes support clinical teams in delivering coordinated, evidence-based care while ensuring comprehensive documentation meets both clinical and regulatory requirements.
