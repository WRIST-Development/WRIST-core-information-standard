# Generic Personas The following personas represent key stakeholders in the Core Information Standard implementation: | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | Clinical Practitioner | Healthcare professional providing direct patient care | Clinical assessment, care planning, clinical coding, system navigation | | CISV2.P2 | Care Coordinator | Professional coordinating care across multiple services | Multi-disciplinary working, communication, care pathway knowledge | | CISV2.P3 | Social Worker | Social care professional conducting assessments and support planning | Assessment skills, legal knowledge, person-centred planning | | CISV2.P4 | Administrative Staff | Non-clinical staff managing data entry and records | Data entry accuracy, understanding of data requirements | | CISV2.P5 | Service User/Patient | Person receiving care and support services | Self-advocacy, understanding of their needs and preferences | | CISV2.P6 | Family Carer | Family member or friend providing unpaid support | Understanding of person's needs, communication with professionals | | CISV2.P7 | System Administrator | Technical staff managing system configuration and maintenance | Technical expertise, data governance, security management | | CISV2.P8 | Information Analyst | Professional analysing data for service improvement | Data analysis, reporting, quality improvement methodologies | ## Persona Details **Clinical Practitioners** need intuitive interfaces that support clinical workflow while ensuring comprehensive data capture. **Care Coordinators** require visibility across services and the ability to track care plan progress. **Service Users** must be supported to contribute their voice and preferences effectively to their records.
