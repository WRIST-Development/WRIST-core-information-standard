## Business Processes The Core Information Standard supports key business processes across health and social care settings. These processes ensure consistent information capture, sharing and use throughout a person's care journey. ### Process Overview The following processes represent critical workflows where the Core Information Standard enables effective information management: - **Initial Registration and Assessment** - Capturing foundational person information and initial care needs - **Care Planning and Review** - Developing and maintaining person-centered care plans - **Clinical Encounter Documentation** - Recording consultations, assessments and interventions - **Medication Management** - Safe prescribing, review and reconciliation processes - **Referral and Transition** - Managing care transfers between services and settings - **Safeguarding Response** - Identifying and managing safeguarding concerns - **Legal Status Management** - Maintaining current legal information and capacity assessments - **End of Life Planning** - Documenting preferences and anticipatory care decisions
