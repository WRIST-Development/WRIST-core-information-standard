## Clinical Decision Tables The Core Information Standard incorporates several key decision support mechanisms to guide safe and effective care delivery. ### Mental Capacity Assessment Decision Logic person's presentation. | Condition | Required Elements | Output Action | |---|---|---| | Person lacks capacity for specific decision AND no valid LPA/deputy in place | CISV2.DE257 (Mental capacity (LPA details), CISV2.DE293 (LPA name), CISV2.DE294 (Contact details) | Route decisions to attorney, document consultation | | Person under Mental Health Act detention | CISV2.DE326 (MHA status), decision if applicable) | Proceed under best interests, document rationale, assess capacity when feasible | ### Safeguarding Alert Decision Logic **Title:** Safeguarding Concern Escalation
