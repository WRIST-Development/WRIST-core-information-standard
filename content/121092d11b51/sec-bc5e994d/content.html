## Mental Capacity Assessment Decision **Purpose**: Determine appropriate consent pathway based on capacity assessment | Condition | Output Action | |---|---| | Person has capacity for specific decision | Obtain direct consent from person | | Person lacks capacity + Valid LPA exists | Consult LPA for decision | | Person lacks capacity + No LPA | Follow best interests process with MDT | | Emergency + Unable to assess | Act in best interests, document rationale | ## Reasonable Adjustments Requirement **Purpose**: Identify when reasonable adjustments must be provided | Condition | Output Action | |---|---| | Communication impairment identified | Provide appropriate communication support | | Physical disability affecting access | Ensure accessible facilities/equipment | | Learning disability | Provide easy-read materials, allow extra time | | Sensory impairment | Provide appropriate format (large print, audio, BSL) | ## Safeguarding Referral Decision **Purpose**: Determine when safeguarding referral required | Condition | Output Action | |---|---| | Current abuse/neglect suspected | Immediate safeguarding referral | | Historical abuse disclosed | Assess current risk, consider referral | | Self-neglect indicators | Adult safeguarding assessment | | Child at risk identified | Children's services referral |
