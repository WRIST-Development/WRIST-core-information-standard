The Core Information Standard enables key healthcare processes through integrated data flows that support person-centered care delivery. These processes span initial registration through ongoing care coordination, with each workflow designed to capture and share essential information across the care continuum. ## Process Overview The standard supports five primary business processes that form the foundation of integrated care delivery: - **Initial Person Registration**: Establishing the individual's core record with demographics, contacts, and initial requirements - **Comprehensive Assessment**: Capturing clinical, social, and personal information to build a complete picture of needs - **Care Planning Development**: Creating person-centered plans based on assessments and individual goals - **Ongoing Care Coordination**: Managing referrals, appointments, and service delivery across multiple providers - **Emergency Response**: Accessing critical information and executing contingency plans during urgent situations
