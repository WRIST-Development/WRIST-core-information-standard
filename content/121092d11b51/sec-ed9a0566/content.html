## Generic Personas ### System User Personas | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | GP Practice Administrator | Manages patient registration and demographics | Data entry, PDS integration, consent management | | CISV2.P2 | Hospital Clinician | Reviews and updates clinical information during encounters | Clinical assessment, risk evaluation, care planning | | CISV2.P3 | Community Nurse | Provides care in community settings, updates care records | Home visits, medication administration, care coordination | | CISV2.P4 | Social Worker | Manages safeguarding and social care assessments | Safeguarding procedures, mental capacity assessment, care packages | | CISV2.P5 | Care Coordinator | Oversees integrated care delivery across settings | Multi-agency coordination, care plan reviews, service integration | | CISV2.P6 | Person/Carer | Individual receiving care or their representative | Self-advocacy, care preferences, consent provision | Each persona interacts with different aspects of the Core Information Standard based on their role and responsibilities within the care system.
