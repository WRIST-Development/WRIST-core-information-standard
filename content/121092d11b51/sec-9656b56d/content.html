The Core Information Standard Version 2.0 supports essential business processes across health and care settings. These workflows ensure consistent information capture and sharing to enable integrated person-centred care delivery. Each process maps key activities performed by health and care professionals, identifying data requirements and interdependencies. The following processes represent common patterns of care delivery, from initial patient registration through ongoing care coordination. Each workflow identifies the personas involved, data elements accessed or collected, and decision points that guide care pathways.
