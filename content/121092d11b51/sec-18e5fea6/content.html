**Description:** Determines required adjustments based on person's impairments and needs | Condition | Required Action | |---|---| | Hearing impairment + Prefers BSL | Provide BSL interpreter for appointments | | Visual impairment + Uses braille | Provide information in braille format | | Autism spectrum + Sensory sensitivity | Offer quiet environment, minimise waiting | | Learning disability + Needs support | Allow extra time, provide easy-read materials | | Mobility impairment + Uses wheelchair | Ensure wheelchair access, adjustable furniture | | Communication difficulty + Uses AAC | Support use of communication aids | ### Table 2: Safeguarding Escalation **Description:** Guides safeguarding response based on identified concerns | Risk Indicators | Immediate Actions | |---|---| | immediate harm risk | Ensure safety, contact emergency services if needed | | Self-neglect affecting health | Assess capacity, involve mental health team | | Domestic abuse disclosed | Provide safety information, MARAC referral if high risk | ### Table 3: Medication Review Triggers **Description:** Identifies when medication reviews are required | Condition | Review Requirement | |---|---| | Age
