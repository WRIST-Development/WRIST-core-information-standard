## Key Clinical Recommendations The Core Information Standard supports evidence-based healthcare delivery through structured data capture and sharing. Key intervention areas include: | Intervention | Recommendation | Source | Grade | |---|---|---|---| | Person-centred care planning | Implement structured care planning with person involvement | NICE Guidelines | A | | Information sharing consent | Record explicit consent for information sharing | Data Protection Act 2018 | A | | Reasonable adjustments | Document required accessibility adjustments | Equality Act 2010 | A | | Medication reconciliation | Maintain accurate medication records with structured dosing | BNF/NICE | A | | Risk assessment | Conduct regular risk assessments with mitigation plans | CQC Standards | A | | Advance care planning | Record advance decisions and preferred place of care | Gold Standards Framework | B | | Safeguarding procedures | Implement safeguarding protocols for vulnerable persons | SCIE Guidelines | A | ## Implementation Context These interventions are supported through standardised data collection enabling consistent care delivery, quality monitoring, and outcome measurement across healthcare settings.
