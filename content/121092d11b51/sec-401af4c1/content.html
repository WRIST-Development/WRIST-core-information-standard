## Purpose and Scope The Core Information Standard (CISV2) provides a comprehensive framework for capturing, structuring, and sharing essential health and care information about individuals across the UK health and social care system. Developed using WRIST (Wonderful Ramsey Systems Information Specification Tool), this standard establishes a common data model that enables interoperability between different health and care systems. ## Background This standard addresses the critical need for consistent information sharing across health and social care settings. It encompasses person demographics, clinical information, care planning, legal requirements, and administrative data necessary for delivering coordinated care. The model supports both acute and community care scenarios, mental health services, and social care provision. ## Document Overview This Digital Adaptation Kit (DAK) provides implementation guidance for the Core Information Standard v2.0, including business processes, data specifications, decision logic, and functional requirements. The standard covers over 40 major information domains from basic demographics to complex care planning and end-of-life care provisions.
