## Introduction The Core Information Standard Version 2 (CIS V2) defines the essential data elements required for effective health and care information exchange across the UK. This standard ensures consistent capture and sharing of person-centred information to support integrated care delivery. ### Purpose and Scope This Digital Adaptation Kit (DAK) provides implementation guidance for the Core Information Standard V2. It covers the fundamental information requirements for person demographics, care preferences, clinical data, and care coordination across health and social care settings. The standard applies to all digital systems that need to exchange person-centred information within the UK health and care ecosystem. ### Background Developed through extensive stakeholder consultation, CIS V2 builds upon previous standards to provide a comprehensive framework for information sharing. It addresses the need for consistent, structured data capture while maintaining flexibility for local requirements and person-centred care approaches.
