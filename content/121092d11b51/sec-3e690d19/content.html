## Purpose and Scope This Digital Adaptation Kit (DAK) documents the Core Information Standard v2.0, a comprehensive framework for person-centred health and social care information sharing. The standard facilitates seamless data exchange across health and social care settings while maintaining privacy, dignity, and person-centred approaches to care delivery. The Core Information Standard encompasses demographic data, clinical information, care planning, and social care elements essential for coordinated care delivery. It supports the NHS Long Term Plan's vision of integrated care and enables professionals to access complete, accurate information when and where it's needed. ## Document Overview This DAK provides implementers with the necessary guidance to adopt and deploy the Core Information Standard effectively. It includes data element specifications, process workflows, decision support logic, and performance indicators to ensure consistent implementation across organisations. The standard was developed using WRIST (Wonderful Ramsey Systems Information Specification Tool) and aligns with UK Core FHIR R4 profiles, SNOMED CT clinical terminology, and NHS Data Dictionary standards.
