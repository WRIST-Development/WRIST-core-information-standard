## Decision-Support Logic ### Table 1: Consent for Information Sharing **Description:** Determines appropriate information sharing based on consent status and capacity | Consent Status | Mental Capacity | Legitimate Interest | Output Action | |---|---|---|---| | Explicit consent given | Has capacity | N/A | Share full record | | No consent given | Has capacity | No | Do not share | | No consent given | Has capacity | Yes (emergency) | Share minimum necessary | | Unable to consent | Lacks capacity | Best interests decision documented | Share per best interests | | Consent withdrawn | Has capacity | No | Stop sharing immediately | ### Table 2: Reasonable Adjustment Requirements **Description:** Determines required adjustments based on impairment type | Impairment Type | Communication Need | Environmental Need | Output Action | |---|---|---|---| | Visual impairment | Large print/audio | Good lighting, clear signage | Provide accessible formats | | Hearing impairment | BSL interpreter | Hearing loop | Book interpreter, ensure equipment | | Learning disability | Easy read | Quiet space, extra time | Prepare easy read materials | | Autism | Clear, direct communication | Minimal sensory stimulation | Adjust environment | | Physical disability | Standard | Wheelchair access | Ensure accessible facilities | ### Table 3: Safeguarding Escalation **Description:** Determines safeguarding response based on risk indicators | Risk Type | Severity | Person Consent | Output Action | |---|---|---|---| | Self-harm risk | High | Refuses support | Immediate mental health referral | | Neglect indicators | Moderate | Agrees to support | Social services assessment | | Financial abuse | Any level | N/A | Adult safeguarding referral | | Child at risk | Any level | N/A | Immediate child protection referral |
