## Healthcare User Personas The following personas represent key stakeholders in healthcare information systems: | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | General Practitioner | Primary care physician managing comprehensive patient records | Clinical assessment, care planning, prescribing, referral management | | CISV2.P2 | Community Nurse | Healthcare professional providing care in community settings | Care coordination, medication administration, assessment documentation | | CISV2.P3 | Hospital Clinician | Acute care specialist managing inpatient and outpatient services | Clinical decision-making, procedure documentation, discharge planning | | CISV2.P4 | Social Worker | Social care professional coordinating support services | Assessment, care planning, safeguarding, multi-agency working | | CISV2.P5 | Care Coordinator | Professional managing care transitions and service coordination | Care planning, resource coordination, communication facilitation | | CISV2.P6 | Administrative Staff | Personnel managing healthcare records and appointments | Data entry, record maintenance, appointment scheduling | **General Practitioners** require comprehensive access to all patient information for holistic care management. **Community Nurses** focus on care delivery documentation and coordination. **Social Workers** emphasise assessment and support planning capabilities.
