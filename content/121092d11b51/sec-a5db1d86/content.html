## System User Personas The following personas represent key users of systems implementing the Core Information Standard v2.0: | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | **GP/Primary Care Physician** | Doctor providing first-contact care, managing chronic conditions, and coordinating referrals. Responsible for maintaining comprehensive patient records. | Clinical assessment, care coordination, medication management, SNOMED CT coding, safeguarding responsibilities | | CISV2.P2 | **Community Nurse** | Registered nurse delivering care in community settings, conducting assessments, administering medications, and supporting care plans. | Clinical procedures, medication administration, care plan implementation, risk assessment, wound care | | CISV2.P3 | **Social Worker** | Professional assessing social care needs, arranging support services, and ensuring safeguarding compliance. Key role in mental capacity assessments. | Social care assessment, safeguarding procedures, mental capacity assessment, care package coordination | | CISV2.P4 | **Hospital Consultant** | Senior doctor managing complex conditions, making treatment decisions, and overseeing specialty care including end-of-life planning. | Specialist clinical knowledge, treatment planning, multidisciplinary coordination, prognostication | | CISV2.P5 | **Care Coordinator** | Professional coordinating integrated care across health and social services, ensuring care plan implementation and review. | Care pathway management, service coordination, review scheduling, stakeholder communication | | CISV2.P6 | **Allied Health Professional** | Therapist (OT/Physio/SLT) providing specialist assessments, interventions, and equipment recommendations. | Functional assessment, therapy planning, equipment prescription, goal setting | | CISV2.P7 | **Care Home Manager** | Registered manager overseeing residential care delivery, ensuring regulatory compliance and coordinating with external services. | Care quality management, regulatory compliance, staff supervision, external liaison | | CISV2.P8 | **Person/Patient Representative** | Individual receiving care or their legitimate representative (LPA holder, parent) participating in care decisions and providing personal information. | Self-advocacy, consent provision, personal preference expression, care participation | Each persona interacts with different sections of the standard based on their professional responsibilities and access permissions.
