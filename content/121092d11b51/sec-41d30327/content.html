## Health Interventions & Recommendations CISV2 supports comprehensive recording of health interventions across care settings. Key recommendations focus on ensuring complete, accurate, and accessible information capture. ### Key Recommendations | Intervention | Recommendation | Source | Grade | |---|---|---|---| | Person Demographics | Capture NHS number with verification status for all records | NHS Data Dictionary | Mandatory | | Reasonable Adjustments | Record all adjustments per Equality Act 2010 requirements | Accessible Information Standard | Required | | Medications Management | Use dm+d coding for all medication entries | NICE Medicines Optimisation | Required | | Care Planning | Document goals, actions and outcomes with person agreement | NICE Guidelines NG86 | Recommended | | Safeguarding | Flag all safeguarding concerns with clear escalation pathways | NHS Safeguarding Guidelines | Mandatory | | Mental Capacity | Record capacity assessments for relevant decisions | Mental Capacity Act 2005 | Required | | End of Life Care | Document CPR decisions and preferred place of care/death | NHS End of Life Care | Required | ### Implementation Context These interventions support integrated care delivery by ensuring critical information is available at the point of care. The standard mandates structured data capture using established terminologies (SNOMED CT, dm+d) while allowing free text for person-specific details.
