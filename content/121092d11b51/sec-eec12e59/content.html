## Purpose and Scope The Core Information Standard Version 2.0 represents a foundational framework for capturing and sharing essential person-centered information across health and social care settings in the UK. This standard defines the minimum dataset required to support safe, effective care delivery while promoting interoperability between diverse care systems. ## Background Developed through extensive stakeholder consultation, this standard addresses the critical need for consistent, comprehensive information capture across care transitions. It encompasses demographic details, clinical information, care planning elements, and person-centered preferences to ensure holistic care delivery. The standard aligns with UK regulatory requirements including the Equality Act 2010, Mental Capacity Act 2005, and NHS data standards.
