## Generic Personas The following personas represent key users and stakeholders of the Core Information Standard. | Persona ID | Title | Description | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | General Practitioner | Primary care physician managing ongoing patient care | Clinical assessment, care coordination, medication management, SNOMED CT coding | | CISV2.P2 | Social Worker | Professional coordinating social care services and safeguarding | Needs assessment, safeguarding procedures, care planning, multi-agency working | | CISV2.P3 | Community Nurse | Nurse providing care in home/community settings | Clinical procedures, medication administration, care planning, wound management | | CISV2.P4 | Care Home Manager | Manager overseeing residential care provision | Care quality oversight, regulatory compliance, staff management, care planning | | CISV2.P5 | Hospital Consultant | Specialist physician providing acute/specialist care | Specialist diagnostics, treatment planning, multidisciplinary coordination | | CISV2.P6 | Person/Patient | Individual receiving health and social care services | Self-advocacy, care preferences, consent provision, care plan participation | | CISV2.P7 | Family Carer | Family member providing informal care and support | Personal care, medication support, advocacy, care coordination | | CISV2.P8 | Information Manager | Professional managing health/care information systems | Data quality, system integration, information governance, technical standards | ### Persona Context These personas interact with CISV2 data throughout the care journey. The standard must support their diverse needs while maintaining person-centred principles and ensuring appropriate access controls based on role and consent.
