These tables reference specific data elements to trigger appropriate clinical actions and safety checks. ### DT-01: Medication Safety Decision Support **Description**: Automated decision support for active medication with same therapeutic class | Warn of potential duplication, suggest review | | High-risk medication | CISV2.DE1330 (Medication name), CISV2.DE10 (Date of birth) | IF high-risk drug warning, suggest monitoring | ### DT-02: Safeguarding Risk Assessment **Description**: Automated risk identification for vulnerable patients requiring additional safeguarding measures | Condition |
