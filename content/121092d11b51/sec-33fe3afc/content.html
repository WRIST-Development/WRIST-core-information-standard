## Implementation Personas | Persona ID | Occupational Title | Description | Targeted | Key Competencies | |---|---|---|---|---| | CISV2.P1 | Clinical Data Manager | Manages clinical data standards and governance | Yes | Data modelling, clinical terminology, FHIR standards | | CISV2.P2 | Health Informatics Specialist | Designs and implements health information systems | Yes | System integration, interoperability, HL7 FHIR | | CISV2.P3 | GP Practice Manager | Oversees primary care practice operations | Yes | Clinical workflows, patient data management, NHS requirements | | CISV2.P4 | Social Care Coordinator | Coordinates social care services and planning | Yes | Care planning, assessment tools, multi-agency working | | CISV2.P5 | Hospital Systems Analyst | Analyses and optimises hospital information systems | Yes | Healthcare IT, system analysis, process improvement | | CISV2.P6 | Community Nurse | Provides nursing care in community settings | Yes | Clinical assessment, care coordination, documentation | ### Clinical Data Manager (CISV2.P1) Responsible for ensuring data quality and compliance with clinical standards. They work closely with clinical teams to define data requirements and maintain terminology mappings. Key focus areas include SNOMED CT implementation, data validation rules, and ensuring regulatory compliance across all clinical records. ### Health Informatics Specialist (CISV2.P2) Leads technical implementation of interoperability standards and system integrations. They design APIs, configure FHIR endpoints, and ensure seamless data exchange between systems. Their expertise spans both technical architecture and clinical workflow optimisation. ### GP Practice Manager (CISV2.P3) Oversees implementation of the standard within primary care settings, ensuring it supports existing workflows and meets practice needs. They coordinate training programmes and manage change processes to ensure successful adoption by clinical staff. ### Social Care Coordinator (CISV2.P4) Focuses on care planning and assessment components of the standard, ensuring social care needs are accurately captured and shared. They work with multi-disciplinary teams to implement person-centred planning approaches and outcome measurement frameworks. ### Hospital Systems Analyst (CISV2.P5) Analyses hospital workflows and system requirements to optimise implementation of the standard. They identify integration points, design data flows, and ensure the standard supports clinical decision-making and quality improvement initiatives. ### Community Nurse (CISV2.P6) Provides practical input on clinical data requirements and usability aspects of the standard. They ensure documentation supports clinical practice and contributes to improved patient outcomes through better care coordination and information sharing.
