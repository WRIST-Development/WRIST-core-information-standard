## Key Clinical and Care Interventions The Core Information Standard supports documentation of evidence-based interventions across health and social care settings: | Intervention | Recommendation | Source | Grade | |---|---|---|---| | Person-Centred Care Planning | Develop individualized care plans with person involvement | NICE NG86 | A | | Reasonable Adjustments | Assess and implement adjustments per Equality Act 2010 | Equality Act 2010 | Mandatory | | Mental Capacity Assessment | Conduct assessments where capacity is in doubt | Mental Capacity Act 2005 | Mandatory | | Medication Reconciliation | Review and reconcile medications at care transitions | NICE NG5 | A | | Advance Care Planning | Document preferences for future care | NICE NG142 | B | | Safeguarding Assessment | Identify and respond to safeguarding concerns | Care Act 2014 | Mandatory | | Risk Assessment | Evaluate and document risks to self and others | Local Policy | C | | Immunization Recording | Maintain accurate vaccination records | PHE Guidance | A | These interventions form the foundation for comprehensive care documentation and support evidence-based decision making across all care settings.
