80% within 18 months 3. **CIS.I8** Emergency Information: Target 99% availability 4. **CIS.I13** Appropriate Sharing: Target 100% compliance 5. **CIS.I16** Person Satisfaction: Target 85% quarterly - Annual impact assessment published
