Detailed Persona Descriptions ### P001: General Practitioner (GP) **Dr. Sarah Chen** is an experienced GP partner in a medium-sized practice serving 12,000 patients. She sees 30-40 patients daily across face-to-face and remote consultations. **Key Responsibilities:** - First-contact assessment and diagnosis - Ongoing management of chronic conditions - Preventive care and health screening - Referral to secondary care services - Medication review and prescribing **Information Needs:** - Quick access to comprehensive patient history - Real-time medication and allergy information - Hospital discharge summaries and specialist letters - Safeguarding alerts and care plans - Ability to update problem lists and care plans efficiently **System Interaction:** - Primary user of read/write functions - Requires rapid data entry during 10-minute consultations - Needs clinical decision support for prescribing and referrals ### P002: Practice Nurse **James Thompson** is a practice nurse specializing in diabetes and respiratory care, conducting structured chronic disease reviews. **Key Responsibilities:** - Chronic disease monitoring and management - Immunization delivery - Health assessments and screening - Patient education and self-management support - Care plan development and review **Information Needs:** - Disease-specific templates and protocols - Historical test results and trends - Care plan goals and progress tracking - Patient preference and adjustment information **System Interaction:** - Heavy user of structured data entry forms - Requires integration with point-of-care devices - Needs automated recall and reminder systems ### P003: Hospital Doctor **Mr. Ahmed Patel** is a consultant physician in elderly care medicine, managing complex multi-morbid patients. **Key Responsibilities:** - Comprehensive geriatric assessment - Acute medical management - Discharge planning and coordination - Multidisciplinary team leadership **Information Needs:** - GP referral information and community care context - Complete medication history from all sources - Functional and cognitive assessment results - Social circumstances and support networks - Advance care planning documentation **System Interaction:** - Requires mobile access for ward rounds - Needs to view information from multiple sources - Must document complex assessments efficiently ### P004: Ward Nurse **Sister Mary O'Brien** leads a busy medical ward team providing 24/7 care for acutely ill patients. **Key Responsibilities:** - Medication administration and monitoring - Personal care coordination - Patient observation and assessment - Discharge preparation - Family communication **Information Needs:** - Real-time care plans and task lists - Allergy and risk alerts - Reasonable adjustment requirements - Contact details for next of kin - Discharge planning information **System Interaction:** - Uses bedside terminals and mobile devices - Requires quick documentation during care delivery - Needs clear visual alerts and warnings ### P005: Social Worker **Rebecca Williams** is a senior social worker in the integrated discharge team, coordinating complex discharges. **Key Responsibilities:** - Family dynamics and support systems - Financial and housing circumstances - Previous care packages and outcomes **System Interaction:** - Requires access from multiple locations - Needs to share information with external agencies - Must maintain detailed case notes ### P006: Community Nurse **David Lee** is a district nurse providing complex care in patients' homes across a rural area. **Key Responsibilities:** - Wound care and clinical procedures - Needs voice input options ### P007: Care Home Manager **Patricia Brown** manages a 60-bed residential care home for older adults with dementia. **Key Responsibilities:** - Care quality oversight - Regulatory compliance - Staff training records - GP visit records and hospital communications **System Interaction:** - Oversees data quality and completeness - Generates regulatory reports - Manages access permissions for staff ### P008: Patient/Person **Mrs. Ethel Johnson**, 78, lives alone with multiple chronic conditions including diabetes, heart failure, and mild cognitive impairment. **Key Responsibilities:** -
