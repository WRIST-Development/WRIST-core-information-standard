appointment systems, confidentiality | ## Detailed Persona Profiles ### P001: General Practitioner (GP) Dr. Sarah Chen is a GP partner with 15 years of experience. She manages a patient list of 1,800 people, seeing 30-40 patients daily. Her responsibilities include diagnosing and treating acute and chronic conditions, coordinating care with specialists, and leading practice quality improvement initiatives. **Key interactions with Core Information Standard:** - Reviews and updates problem lists during consultations - Documents medication changes and monitors for interactions - Records advance care planning discussions - Updates social circumstances affecting health - Creates and reviews care plans for complex patients ### P002: Practice Nurse James Morrison is a practice nurse specializing in chronic disease management. He runs diabetes, asthma, and hypertension clinics, administers immunizations, and performs health checks. **Key interactions with Core Information Standard:** - Records vital signs and clinical observations - Updates immunization records - Documents lifestyle factors (smoking, alcohol, exercise) - Identifies and records need for reasonable adjustments - Contributes to care plan development ### P003: Hospital Consultant Professor Amira Patel is a consultant geriatrician managing complex elderly patients. She leads a multidisciplinary team and makes critical decisions about treatment escalation and discharge planning. **Key interactions with Core Information Standard:** - Reviews comprehensive patient information for clinical decisions - Documents capacity assessments and best interest decisions - Updates advance care planning and DNACPR status - Coordinates complex discharge plans - Leads multidisciplinary team meetings ### P006: Social Worker Michael O'Brien is a hospital social worker conducting assessments for patients requiring social care support. He coordinates with multiple agencies to arrange appropriate care packages. **Key interactions with Core Information Standard:** - Completes comprehensive social care assessments - Documents safeguarding concerns and actions - Records care package details and funding arrangements - Updates information about informal carers - Assesses and documents mental capacity for care decisions ### P011: Patient/Person Maria Rodriguez is a 72-year-old with diabetes, heart failure, and mild cognitive impairment. She wants to maintain independence and be involved in care decisions. **Key interactions with Core Information Standard:** - Contributes to "About Me" section - Reviews and corrects demographic information - Provides consent for information sharing - Expresses preferences for care and treatment - Identifies important relationships and support needs ## Persona Interaction Patterns ### Cross-Setting Collaboration - **GP ↔ Hospital Consultant**: Referral information, discharge summaries, medication changes - **Community Nurse ↔ Social Worker**: Joint assessments, care coordination - **Care Home Manager ↔ GP**: Resident health updates, medication reviews - **Patient ↔ All Professionals**: Preference communication, consent, care planning participation ### Information Priority by Persona - **Emergency Clinicians**: Allergies, current medications, advance directives, key contacts - **Social Care Staff**: Social circumstances, support networks, safeguarding, capacity - **Primary Care**: Comprehensive view including preventive care, chronic conditions, social factors - **Patients/Carers**: Understanding conditions, care plans, appointment schedules, support services
