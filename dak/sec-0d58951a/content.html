text | BP005 | Person reported | | PREF010 | Personal Care | Washing/dressing preferences | text | R | Free text | BP005 | Person reported | ## Data Quality and Governance ### Validation Rules - **Mandatory fields** must be completed before record can be saved - **Coded fields** must use specified terminology where available - **Dates** cannot be future dates unless specifically allowed (e.g., review dates) - **Clinical measurements** must fall within clinically plausible ranges - **Cross-field validation** ensures consistency (e.g., end date after start date) ### Terminology Bindings - **SNOMED CT** - Primary clinical terminology - **dm+d** - All UK medications and devices - **LOINC** - Laboratory and observation codes - **NHS Data Dictionary** - Administrative codes - **Local codes** - Where national codes unavailable ### Privacy and Security - **Role-based access** controls who can view/edit elements - **Audit trail** required for all data changes - **Consent flags** control information sharing - **Sensitive data** (e.g., mental health, sexual health) requires additional controls
