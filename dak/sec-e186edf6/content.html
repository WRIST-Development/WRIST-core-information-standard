## BP-001: Comprehensive Geriatric Assessment (CGA) **Process ID**: BP-001 **Process Title**: Comprehensive Geriatric Assessment Comprehensive Geriatric Assessment is a multidimensional, interdisciplinary diagnostic process to determine the medical, psychological, and functional capabilities of an older person. This process aims to develop a coordinated and integrated plan for treatment and long-term follow-up.
