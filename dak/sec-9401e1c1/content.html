## Overview This section specifies the functional and non-functional requirements for systems implementing the WRIST Core Information Standard. These requirements ensure that digital health solutions deliver the capabilities needed for comprehensive, integrated care while meeting essential quality attributes for security, performance, and usability. ## Functional Requirements Functional requirements define what the system must do to support WRIST-based workflows and data management. | Requirement ID | Title | Description | Linked
