Annual reporting of system carbon footprint with reduction targets | Environmental | Low | ## Requirement Dependencies and Constraints ### Critical Dependencies - NHS Identity Management integration for authentication - SNOMED CT and dm+d licensing for clinical coding - FHIR UK Core specifications for interoperability - Local information governance approval for data sharing ### Key Constraints - Must maintain separate instances for each ICS/region due to governance - Cannot modify core WRIST data model without national approval - Must support transition from legacy systems with data migration - Subject to NHS Digital clinical safety assessment process
