and WRIST Implementation ### Preventive Interventions WRIST supports population health management through structured capture of: - **Immunization programs** via the Vaccinations section, including batch numbers, sites, and adverse events - **Screening activities** recorded as Procedures with appropriate SNOMED CT coding - **Health promotion** documented in Contacts with Professionals and Plan sections - **Risk stratification** using Assessment scores and Problem List analysis ### Diagnostic Interventions The standard's comprehensive approach to diagnostics includes: - **Investigation ordering** through Investigations Requested with clinical indications - **Result management** via Investigation Results with structured values and abnormal flags - **Clinical examination** findings with detailed body site localization - **Assessment tools** supporting validated instruments (e.g., cognitive assessments, frailty scores) ### Therapeutic Interventions #### Pharmacological Interventions WRIST's Medications and Medical Devices section enables: - **Prescribing** with structured dose instructions and dm+d coding - **Medication reconciliation** across care transitions - **Adverse reaction tracking** linked to Allergies and Adverse Reactions - **Discontinuation management** with reasons and outcomes recorded - **Medical device management** including implants and durable equipment #### Non-Pharmacological Interventions The standard supports diverse therapeutic approaches: - **Psychological therapies** documented in Procedures and Therapies - **Rehabilitation programs** tracked through Care and Support Plans - **Social prescribing** via Plan and Requested Actions - **Lifestyle interventions** captured in Social Context and monitoring ### Care Coordination Interventions WRIST excels at supporting integrated care through: - **Referral management** with detailed clinical information transfer - **Multidisciplinary team coordination** via shared Problem Lists and Care Plans - **Transition planning** using Admission/Discharge Details - **Case management** through comprehensive Professional Contacts tracking ### Emergency and Urgent Care Interventions Critical interventions are supported by: - **Safety alerts** for high-risk conditions or allergies - **Emergency care plans** in Contingency/Safety Plans section - **Resuscitation decisions** clearly documented in End of Life Care - **Crisis response** protocols in Additional Support Plans ## Implementation Considerations Successful implementation of these interventions requires: 1. **Clinical engagement** to ensure interventions align with local pathways 2. **Technical infrastructure** supporting real-time data capture and sharing 3. **Training programs** for all user personas on intervention documentation 4. **Quality assurance** processes monitoring intervention completeness 5. **Outcome tracking** using WRIST's indicator framework The comprehensive nature of WRIST ensures that all recommended interventions can be properly documented, monitored, and evaluated within a single coherent information model.
