Dashboard and Reporting ### Real-Time Monitoring - **Safety alerts dashboard** - Live view of triggered alerts and responses - **Data quality monitor** - Real-time completeness and accuracy metrics - **User activity tracker** - Current system usage and performance ### Regular Reports - **Weekly operational report** - Key metrics for clinical teams - **Monthly quality report** - Comprehensive quality and safety analysis - **Quarterly strategic report** - Progress against strategic objectives - **Annual outcomes report** - Population health impact assessment ### Benchmarking - Compare performance across teams, departments, and organizations - National and regional benchmark participation - Continuous improvement targets based on top quartile performance - Sharing best practices from high performers
