## Overview The WRIST Core Information Standard enables comprehensive performance monitoring through structured data capture across all aspects of health and social care. These indicators support quality improvement, regulatory compliance, and population health management. Implementation Guidance Successful indicator monitoring requires: 1. **Automated calculation** using WRIST data elements with minimal manual intervention 2. **Real-time dashboards** for operational indicators (daily/weekly) 3. **Statistical process control** charts for tracking improvement 4. **Drill-down capability** from indicators to patient lists for action 5. **Role-based access** ensuring appropriate visibility 6. **Regular validation** of indicator definitions against changing standards 7. **Integration with reporting** to regulators and commissioners
